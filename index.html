Uniting the Equestrian Voice of the East End

Who We Are

The Hamptons Equestrian Group is a coalition of equestrian property owners, professionals, riders, and enthusiasts who call the East End home. We’ve come together to protect what we love — our horses, our farms, and the future of our equestrian community.

Join the Group

Why We Exist

In the past 20–30 years, many local horse farms have shut down — pushed out by development pressures, zoning restrictions, and lack of public support. As individuals, it’s hard to push back. As a group, we have power.

Make a Difference

What We Do

We advocate for equestrian-friendly policies, raise awareness about the horse industry’s value, promote land preservation, and build a strong support network. We share resources, highlight local events, and create visibility for our community’s needs.

Get Involved

Our Vision

We envision a Hamptons where horse farms thrive, open space is protected, and equestrian traditions endure. We want to ensure that horses remain a vital part of East End life for generations to come.
